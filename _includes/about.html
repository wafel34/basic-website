About the apartment


This spacious 2-bedroom apartment, which sleeps 6, is located within the heart of tourist London. A stone's throw from the British Museum, the British Library, Covent Garden and its theatres, Waterloo Bridge and many more London landmarks.

This modern flat comprises all the latest conveniences including a dishwasher, 'Mile' washing machine, tumble dryer, fridge-freezer, cable flat screen TV and free wireless internet access.

Located on the second floor (no lift), this modern, sunny apartment is comfortable, clean and very practical.
